Account & Data Deletion

App / Developer: Adomet Games

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and personal data associated with any game or application published by Adomet Games. We provide two ways to delete your account: directly from within the app, or by sending us an email.

Important: Account deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your in-game progress, gold, powerups, purchases, and other associated data cannot be restored.

1. Delete Your Account From Within the App

The fastest way to delete your account is from within the game:

  1. Open the game on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings (gear) icon on the main screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  4. Read the confirmation message carefully and tap "Confirm Delete".
  5. Your account and associated data will be deleted from our servers within a reasonable time, typically immediately.

2. Delete Your Account by Email

If you no longer have the app installed, or prefer to request deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to adometgame@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. In the email body, please include:
    • The name of the game/app you used (e.g., "Snake Escape")
    • Your linked email or display name (if known)
    • A brief confirmation that you want your account and data deleted
  4. We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

3. What Data Is Deleted

Upon successful deletion of your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our active systems:

Data TypeDescription
Account identifierThe unique user ID issued by Google Sign-In, Sign in with Apple, or Firebase Authentication.
Email addressThe email address linked to your sign-in provider (if provided).
Display name & profile pictureDisplay name and avatar URL shared by your sign-in provider (if provided).
In-game progressLevel progress, completion status, scores, and achievements.
Virtual currency & itemsGold balance, powerups (e.g., Hint, Bomb, Add Life, Blackhole), and other in-game items.
Gameplay statisticsTotal play count, total play time, completed/failed levels, and similar metrics.
Linked device identifiersFirebase Installation IDs and other identifiers tied to your account.

4. What Data May Be Retained (and For How Long)

Some data may be retained for limited periods after account deletion, only where required by law, for fraud prevention, or for legitimate business purposes:

Data TypeReasonRetention Period
Purchase & transaction recordsTax, accounting, and legal obligationsUp to 10 years, as required by applicable law
Anonymized analyticsAggregated, non-identifiable statistics that cannot be linked back to youRetained indefinitely in anonymized form
Crash logs & security logsDiagnostics, debugging, and fraud preventionUp to 90 days, after which they are deleted or anonymized
Backup archivesStandard backup rotationUp to 30 days, then permanently deleted

None of the retained data can be used to identify you personally after account deletion.

5. Third-Party Account Removal

Deleting your account in our game does not delete your Google or Apple account itself. To manage your Google or Apple account, visit:

  • Google Account: https://myaccount.google.com/
  • Apple ID: https://appleid.apple.com/

If you also wish to revoke the permission you granted to our app within your Google or Apple account, you can do so from each provider's security settings.

6. Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion, contact us at adometgame@gmail.com.
