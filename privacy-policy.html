1. What Information Do We Collect?

In Short: We mostly collect non-identifying device and usage data. If you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, we also receive your email address and display name from that provider.

Information you provide to us

If you contact us by email or through an in-game support form, we receive the information you choose to send (e.g., email address, message, screenshots). Account creation is optional and not required to play our games.

Account information (optional)

If you choose to sign in using a third-party identity provider (e.g., Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple), we receive a limited set of information shared by that provider, which may include:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name and profile picture (if provided by the platform)
  • A unique user identifier issued by the provider

We use this information to identify you across devices, sync your in-game progress, and provide customer support. We do not receive your password from these providers. You can sign out or disconnect your linked account at any time from the in-game settings menu.

Information automatically collected

  • Device information: model, manufacturer, OS version, language, time zone, screen resolution.
  • Identifiers: Advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID / Apple IDFA), Firebase Installation IDs, and similar non-personal identifiers used for analytics and advertising.
  • Approximate location: coarse, non-precise location derived from IP address. We do not collect GPS-precise location.
  • Gameplay data: progress, level completion, in-game events, settings, purchase status (we do not collect payment card data).
  • App performance data: crash logs, stack traces, performance metrics, error reports.

This information is primarily needed to maintain the security and operation of our games, fix bugs and crashes, deliver and measure advertising, and conduct internal analytics. We do not knowingly collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone audio, or other sensitive data.

11. Controls for Do-Not-Track & Global Privacy Control

In Short: We honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals for opt-out requests where applicable laws apply.

Most web browsers and some mobile OSes include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") feature. Because there is no industry standard for DNT, we currently do not respond to DNT signals.

Global Privacy Control (GPC): If you use a browser or extension that supports GPC, we will treat this as a valid request to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information for targeted advertising under applicable US state laws (including the California Consumer Privacy Act). Learn more at globalprivacycontrol.org.

13. Opting Out of Personalized Ads

In Short: You can disable personalized advertising at the device level or from inside the game.
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization".
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track".
  • In-game: open the consent prompt again from the in-game Privacy / Settings menu.

14. Account & Data Deletion

In Short: You can delete your account and associated data at any time, directly from the game or by emailing us.

If you signed in with Google or Apple, you can request deletion of your account and all associated data using either of the following methods:

  • In-game: Open the in-game Settings menu and tap "Delete Account". Confirmation is required. Your account, in-game progress, and stored data will be permanently deleted within a reasonable time.
  • By email: Send a deletion request to adometgame@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account. We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days.

The following data will be deleted: your unique account identifier, email address (if provided), display name, in-game progress, in-app purchase history, and any other user-specific data we store. Some data may be retained in anonymized form or where retention is required by law (e.g., financial records for tax purposes).

For full step-by-step instructions and a detailed list of what is deleted vs. retained, see our Account Deletion page.
